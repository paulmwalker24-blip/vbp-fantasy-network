Research Notes

  • The completed 2026 BBU rooms currently use 8 starters in Sleeper: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, and 2 FLEX.
  • Rosters are 18-player draft-and-hold builds with no waivers, trades, pickups, or weekly lineup decisions after the draft.
  • Sleeper automatically uses each manager's best eligible lineup every week, so useful bench players can help without being manually started.
  • Progressive PPR reception scoring is RB 0.5, WR 0.25, and TE 0.75, so TE depth and pass-catching RBs matter.
  • Because BBU also tracks overall season points and weekly high scores across all Union leagues, total scoring matters as much as head-to-head record.
