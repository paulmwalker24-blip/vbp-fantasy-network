# Co-Manager Superflex Redraft

Working concept for a VBP Fantasy Network league where every team is run by a small front office instead of a single manager.

## Core Hook

Every team must have multiple managers. The league should feel like 12 fantasy front offices competing against each other, with built-in draft discussion, waiver debate, start/sit arguments, and shared accountability.

## League Structure

- Format: Redraft
- Teams: 12
- Co-managers: minimum 2 per team, maximum 3 per team
- Buy-in: $50 per team
- Platform: Sleeper
- Draft type: 3RR slow draft
- Draft start: July 15
- Pick timer: 2 hours per pick
- Overnight pause: 10:00 PM CST to 7:00 AM CST

## Roster Settings

Starting lineup:

- 1 QB
- 2 RB
- 2 WR
- 1 TE
- 2 FLEX
- 1 SUPER FLEX

Bench and reserve:

- 5 bench spots
- 1 IR spot
- No kicker
- No defense

The smaller bench is intentional. It should keep waivers active, make depth decisions matter, and prevent teams from stashing too many players.

## Scoring

- Traditional 0.5 PPR
- TE premium
- 6-point passing touchdowns

Recommended TE premium definition:

- RB/WR receptions: 0.5 points
- TE receptions: 1.0 point

## Co-Manager Rules To Consider

- Each team must have at least 2 active co-managers before the draft begins.
- Each team may have no more than 3 co-managers.
- The $50 buy-in is per team, not per manager.
- One manager should be listed as the primary payment and commissioner-contact manager.
- Sleeper actions made by any co-manager count as official team actions.
- Internal disagreements are handled by the team, not the commissioner.
- If a co-manager leaves, the team must keep at least 2 active managers or add a replacement.
