Bracket Redraft Constitution

Official rules for the 60-team bracket redraft format in the VBP Fantasy Network.

Quick League Summary

The VBP Football Bracket League is a 60-team fantasy football tournament made up of five separate 12-team divisions. Each division runs as its own redraft league during the regular season before qualifying teams advance into a 32-team single-elimination playoff bracket.

The format uses Progressive PPR, rewards both division performance and cross-division scoring, and is built for managers who want a larger tournament feel without giving up standard league structure during the regular season.

Constitution

Use the sections below to review league rules before joining.

League Overview
  • Total Teams: 60
  • Divisions: 5 separate 12-team divisions
  • League Format: Redraft tournament
  • Platform: Sleeper
  • Communication: Discord and Sleeper

Each division functions independently during the regular season before top teams advance to the main playoff bracket.

Season Structure
  • Regular Season: Weeks 1–12
  • Playoffs: Weeks 13–17
  • Championship: Week 17
  • Week 18 is not used.
Scoring Format
  • RB: 0.5 PPR
  • WR: 0.25 PPR
  • TE: 0.75 PPR

All other scoring categories follow standard Sleeper settings unless otherwise stated.

Rosters and Lineups
  • Roster Size: 15 players
  • Starting Lineup: 1 QB, 2 RB, 3 WR, 1 TE, 1 FLEX, 1 DEF
  • Bench: 6 players
  • IR Slots: 2

Lineups must be submitted before the kickoff of each player’s NFL game.

Playoff Qualification and Seeding

At the end of the regular season, 32 teams qualify for the playoff tournament.

  • Seeds 1–5 are the five division winners, ranked by record and points scored.
  • Seeds 6–30 are the next 25 teams by record and points scored across all divisions.
  • Seeds 31–32 are the two highest-scoring teams that did not qualify by record.

Wild Card Play-In

Seeds 31 and 32 play in Week 13 for the final entry into the main bracket.

Bracket Structure
  • Round 1 uses reverse seeding (1 vs 30, 2 vs 29, and so on).
  • After Round 1, the bracket reseeds once so the highest remaining seed faces the lowest remaining seed.
  • From Round 2 forward, the bracket becomes static.

Regular-season ties are broken by total points scored, then max points for.

Trades and Free Agency
  • Trade Deadline: End of Week 11
  • Trades are subject to commissioner review for collusion or abuse only.
  • FAAB budget is $200 per team for the full season.
  • Unused FAAB does not carry over.
Prize Structure

All funds are collected and held through LeagueSafe. Payouts scale relative to the season’s buy-in amount.

  • Division Winner: 2× buy-in
  • Champion: Remaining prize pool
  • Runner-Up: 10× buy-in
  • Semifinalists: 4× buy-in
  • Quarterfinalists: Buy-in + $5
Draft Structure

Divisions are designated as either fast-draft or slow-draft divisions before registration.

  • Fast Draft: 2-minute pick timer
  • Slow Draft: 4-hour pick timer
  • Slow Draft Overnight Pause: 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM CST

If managers cannot agree on a draft time, the commissioner will assign one before the season starts.

Lineup Integrity

Managers are expected to set competitive lineups each week.

  • Leaving lineup spots empty
  • Starting players listed as OUT or IR when viable alternatives exist
  • Repeated failure to address bye weeks

First offense results in a warning. Second offense may result in removal.
