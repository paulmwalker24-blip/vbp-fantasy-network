Welcome to the VBP Fantasy Network Bracket Hub.

This Sleeper hub is the main home for the bracket side of the network. If you are here, you are in the right place for the quick overview, the key links, and the next step.

The goal here is simple: give people one clean place to see what the formats are about, ask questions, and get pointed to the best current opening without having to guess which room makes the most sense first.


[PINNED 2 - FORMAT SNAPSHOT]
Current bracket formats:

Dynasty Bracket
- startup format with a long-term build
- built for owners who want a deeper commitment and a bigger format identity
- shared bracket feel across the full field instead of one isolated room

Bracket Redraft
- seasonal format with the same bigger-bracket concept
- established format lane inside the network
- same shared bracket feel across multiple rooms instead of a one-off home league

Both formats are meant to feel bigger than a single league while still staying organized and easy to follow.


[PINNED 3 - HOW IT WORKS]
How it works:

- Tell me which format interests you
- Tell me whether you prefer fast or slow pace
- I will point you to the best current opening

That keeps everything simple, helps the strongest openings build momentum first, and makes it easier to match people to the right room.


[PINNED 4 - WHAT TO EXPECT]
What you can expect here:

- clear format direction
- posted constitutions and public references
- active commissioner communication
- help finding the best fit instead of getting dropped into a random room

If you are just checking things out, that is fine too. You do not need to know every detail before asking questions here.
