# Recruiting Strategy 2026

This is the current marketing pivot for VBP Fantasy Network recruiting.

## Core Shift

Move from catalog marketing to room-closing marketing.

Catalog marketing says: here are all the leagues, links, counts, and rules.

Room-closing marketing says: here is the one room we are closing today, why it is worth joining, and the easiest next action.

The network still matters, but it should usually be the trust layer after the hook, not the first sentence.

## Posting Rules

- Post one lane at a time.
- Put the paid count in the first content line of every recruiting post.
- Lead with the owner need, not the brand name.
- Keep the first three lines strong enough to stand alone.
- Use one join link and one rules link unless the person asks for more.
- Use reply prompts like `redraft`, `bracket`, `fast`, `Foundry`, or `BBU`.
- Rotate posts by room momentum, not by trying to mention every format equally.
- Move people toward the healthiest active room instead of spreading interest across low-count rooms.

## Current Order Of Operations

1. Close Apex first for Bracket Redraft.
2. Keep Redraft 4 visible as the higher-buy-in standalone seasonal room.
3. Use BBU posts only when there is real draft urgency.
4. Use the active Best Ball Gauntlet 2 as a novelty best-ball lane.
5. Push Foundry as the only Dynasty Bracket priority unless someone specifically asks for fast draft.
6. Use Keeper and Pick'em as secondary options when they match a responder's preference.

## Copy Shape

Use this structure for most public posts:

```text
[paid count] for [specific room].

[One or two lines explaining the format hook.]

Join: [one link]
Rules: [one link]

Reply "[keyword]" if you want the quick rundown.
```

## Platform Notes

Facebook:
- Short direct posts work best.
- One room per post.
- Use comments for extra links instead of crowding the body.

Reddit:
- The title should carry the main pitch.
- Avoid sounding like a full network brochure.
- Include payment structure and format basics, but keep the body tight.

Sleeper classifieds:
- Lead with draft timing, buy-in, and spots needed.
- Use fewer links.
- Push the direct invite.

Discord:
- Treat it as a conversion and operations surface.
- Keep pinned recruiting copy current.
- Remove stale filled-room copy quickly.
