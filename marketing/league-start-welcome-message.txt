[LEAGUE START WELCOME MESSAGE]
Use for: posting in a Sleeper league chat when a new VBP Fantasy Network league starts

Welcome to the VBP Fantasy Network.

I appreciate everyone joining. Whether this is your first league with me or you have been in one of my leagues before, the goal is the same: active managers, clear rules, clean communication, secure payouts, and a competitive league that feels organized from the start.

VBP is built as a fantasy football network, not just a single league. I run several formats across the network, including redraft, dynasty, best ball, keeper, chopped, and bracket leagues. The goal is to give managers a place where leagues are structured, rules are written out, payouts are clear, and the same standards carry from one room to the next.

The bracket leagues are the formats I am most passionate about. They are built to give fantasy football a bigger tournament feel while still keeping each individual Sleeper league easy to manage. Instead of every 12-team room ending on its own island, bracket formats connect multiple divisions into a larger playoff race with more teams, more stakes, and more league-wide context.

No matter which format you are in, I want the experience to be competitive, transparent, and worth coming back to. Please stay active, read through the league rules, communicate when needed, and ask questions if anything is unclear.

If you ever want to see the wider network, check openings, or join another format, start here:

https://vbp-fantasy-network.vercel.app/

Glad to have you here. Let's have a great season.
