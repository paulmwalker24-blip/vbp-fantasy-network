Dynasty League Constitution

Official rules and long-term operating procedures for VBP Fantasy Network dynasty leagues.

Quick League Summary

The VBP Dynasty League is a 12-team Sleeper dynasty format built around full roster continuity from year to year. Managers retain players long term, complete an initial startup draft, and then transition into annual rookie drafts beginning in Year 2.

League dues are handled through LeagueSafe, future-year payment is required when trading away rookie picks, and offseason transactions are restricted until the upcoming season is paid. This format is built for long-term team building, active trading, and year-round management.

Constitution

Use the sections below to review league rules before joining.

League Overview

This document establishes the rules, structure, and operating procedures for the VBP Dynasty League.

  • League Type: Dynasty
  • Platform: Sleeper
  • League Size: 12 teams
  • Divisions: None
  • Matchups: One matchup per week

Dynasty leagues operate with full roster continuity year to year unless players are traded, dropped, or otherwise moved through league mechanisms.

Payment Structure
  • Buy-In: $30 per season unless otherwise specified
  • Processor: LeagueSafe
  • A separate LeagueSafe league will be created for each season.
  • Managers trading away future rookie picks must have that future season paid before the trade is processed.
  • Current-season dues must be paid before the startup draft.
  • Future-season dues must be paid before each rookie draft.
League Calendar
  • The fantasy season ends after the Week 17 championship.
  • Teams are temporarily locked after the championship game.
  • Offseason transactions reopen after the upcoming season’s dues are collected.
  • Managers who have not paid remain restricted from transactions until payment is complete.

Restricted transactions include trades, adds, drops, waiver claims, and taxi squad changes.

Startup Draft
  • Platform: Sleeper
  • Format: Snake draft
  • Player Pool: All NFL veterans and incoming rookies from the current NFL Draft class
  • Draft Order: Generated using 100yardrush.com
  • The commissioner will post the results publicly for transparency.
  • Draft timing and clock settings will be announced before the draft.
Annual Rookie Draft
  • Begins in Year 2
  • Format: 4 rounds
  • Type: Linear draft order
  • Eligibility: Rookies from the most recent NFL Draft class only
  • The rookie draft takes place within 7 days of the NFL Draft ending.
  • Upcoming season dues must be paid before the rookie draft.
Trades and Pick Rules

Trading is a core part of dynasty league strategy, including player and rookie-pick movement.

  • Future rookie picks may be traded only if the manager has paid for the corresponding season.
  • Trade review is commissioner-based and intended to prevent collusion or abuse.
  • Trades are not vetoed solely because managers disagree with value.
Offseason Access and Restrictions

At the end of each season, teams that have not paid for the upcoming year may be restricted from offseason activity.

  • No trades
  • No player adds or drops
  • No waiver claims
  • No taxi squad changes

This keeps future roster control tied to paid league participation.

Governance and Commissioner Authority

The commissioner manages payment enforcement, league timing, and procedural communication to keep the dynasty format operating cleanly across seasons.

  • Draft dates and transaction reopening windows are announced by the commissioner.
  • Managers who fail to pay by required deadlines may be replaced before the rookie draft.
  • League procedures are designed to protect competitive balance and long-term continuity.
