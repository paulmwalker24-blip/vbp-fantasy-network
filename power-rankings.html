Ranking Model

How Scores Will Work

Every rankings board should show clean owner scores and when the snapshot was refreshed, with methodology kept in a separate readable section.

Drafted-roster boards publish only after meaningful roster data is available. Pending formats stay visible without pretending a board is already live.

Roster How strong the starters and useful bench pieces are for that format.
Results Points, record, Max PF, or survival status once the season starts.
Path Playoff seed, cut line, division race, or weekly elimination pressure.
Format Verified league scoring, positional scarcity, dynasty age, keeper cost, or chopped floor.
